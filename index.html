WELCOME TO JOJO

JOJO IS YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD BAR RELAXED, SOCIAL AND UNPRETENTIOUS

A place for pre-drinks, casual nights out, and genuine connections.

At JOJO every cocktail is freshly produced. We keg them and serve on tap, so every pour is fast and consistent for the price of only 50 DKK.

And also - we obsess over details. From measurement to garnish, we bring great cocktails to life through skill and dedication.
